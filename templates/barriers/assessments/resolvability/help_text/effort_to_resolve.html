Resource related questions

To help you decide what option to select consider:

  • Is this barrier resolvable through current available resources?
  • How much existing resources will be needed to solve this barrier?
  • Are more resources required to resolve this barrier over time?
  • Does including this barrier in your work programme require reprioritising existing resources?
  • Are there resources outside of government that can be used to resolve the barrier, such as trade associations, political connections of UK businesses to regulators or in-country government?
  • To what extent resolving this barrier will be dependent on ministerial engagements?
