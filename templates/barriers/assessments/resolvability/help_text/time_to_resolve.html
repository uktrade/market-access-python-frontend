Time related questions

To help you decide what option to select consider:

  • How long has the barrier been in place?
  • Why does this barrier exist?
  • How entrenched is the barrier?
  • Has there been previous attempts to remove this barrier and why have they failed?
  • How sensitive is this topic for the host government and how motivated the government is to address this barrier?
  • Do we need to work with other governments to resolve this barrier and are they willing to cooperate?
